IIT Ropar Delegation

First-year B.Tech students from USET, Lamrin Tech Skills University Punjab attended the prestigious IUPAP ICPE-2025 at IIT Ropar (16–20 Dec 2025). The conference highlighted the convergence of AI, Quantum Technologies, and Virtual Labs in modern physics teaching. Guided by Mr. Shubham and Ms. Neha, the visit reflects LTSU’s commitment to experiential learning and academic excellence.
