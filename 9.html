The Night of Rhythm

Raag Ratri

Cultural Kaleidoscope

"Raag Ratri," the Dandiya Night organized by the Cultural Kaleidoscope Club of USET at Lamrin Tech Skills University Punjab, concluded with grand success. The evening was a kaleidoscope of vibrant music, colorful traditional attire, and the infectious festive spirit of Navratri.

Students, faculty, and staff came together to celebrate culture, unity, and joy, creating memories that resonate far beyond the dance circle. It was a true reflection of the vibrant diversity fostered at LTSU.
