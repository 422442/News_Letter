Flagship Event

Hack the Future 2.0: Solving Real-World Challenges

The second edition of Hack the Future pushed the boundaries of student creativity. This 24-hour marathon saw over 40 teams building viable prototypes for sustainable energy, smart healthcare, and fintech solutions.

The event emphasized Commercialization of Innovation. Industry mentors evaluated teams on scalability and user impact. Winners gained a pathway to the USET Incubation Centre.

Patent Registration & Research Milestone

Pioneering Quantum Simulators

USET at Lamrin Tech Skills University, Punjab, announces the formal design registration of the "SMART Quantum Computing Simulation Device" (Design No. 474725-001).

This achievement is credited to Amandeep Kaur (USOC), Pooja Rani (USOC), and Shubham Sharma (USET), bridging theoretical research and industrial application.

The registration reflects our institution's relentless pursuit of excellence in emerging technologies and next-generation computational infrastructure.
