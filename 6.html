Amit Kumar Sah (B.Tech Cybersecurity, 2024 Batch) delivered an impressive performance at the Indian Challenger's Cup. Competing against nearly 7,000 participants from 7 countries, Amit excelled in both Kata and Kumite (Under 85 kg), securing a well-deserved Bronze.
