From the Dean's Desk

As we reflect on the phenomenal journey of the July-December 2025 session, it fills me with immense pride to witness the University School of Engineering & Technology (USET) evolving into a global epicenter for skill-based innovation.

Our collaboration with industry giants like L&T and IBM has moved beyond traditional classrooms into live "Cyber-Physical" laboratories. This semester, we haven't just taught engineering; we have engineered solutions for the future. From our students winning accolades at ISRO to representing the nation in global innovation summits, the spirit of 'Learning by Doing' is vibrant across our campus.

This newsletter is a window into the dedication of our faculty and the boundless potential of our students. We celebrate the patents filed, the hackathons won, and the cultural tapestry that makes USET unique.

Warm Regards,

Prof. (Dr.) H P S Dhami

Dean, School of Engineering & Technology
