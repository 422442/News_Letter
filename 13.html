Powering Industry: Cheema Boilers Ltd

USET students embarked on a specialized technical visit to Cheema Boilers Ltd. Witnessing high-pressure steam generation and industrial boiler manufacturing first-hand, students explored the complex thermodynamics and material sciences that drive global energy infrastructure.

Industry Visit: Sensation Software Solutions

On October 13, 2025, Batch 2024 students from the Cyber Security and Data Science streams of USET, Lamrin Tech Skills University Punjab, visited Sensation Software Solutions (Pvt.) Ltd., Mohali. They explored real-world SDLC workflows, data analytics applications, cybersecurity practices, and IT consulting processes. The visit provided valuable industry exposure, linking classroom learning with practical IT operations.
