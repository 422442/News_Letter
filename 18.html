Mr. Viqaruddin Surki (IBM Delivery Head) welcomed freshers with an inspiring session blending coding, creativity, and innovation. Cyber-Physical Systems:
L&T Learning Program

A comprehensive two-week program led by L&T experts trained students in Microprocessors & Design Frameworks. Electronics and Mechanical students gained hands-on experience in cutting-edge industrial technology, bridging the gap between academic theory and practical application. The workshop concluded with a project showcase demonstrating their new skills.

AI-Driven Future:
YUVAi & Impact Summit

Dr. Sarwan Singh (NIELIT) and industry experts introduced the upcoming India AI Impact Summit 2026. The session highlighted the transformative power of Artificial Intelligence in healthcare, agriculture, and education. Students were encouraged to explore innovative AI solutions for real-world problems and participate in national-level hackathons.

Winning at Work:
Career Readiness

Ms. Anu K. Manhotra led the dynamic 'Winning at Work' Workshop for CSE students, focusing on interview prep, personal branding, and cultivating a growth mindset. Through interactive activities, students created detailed mind maps to visualize their career goals. The session provided actionable strategies to build confidence and professional presence.
