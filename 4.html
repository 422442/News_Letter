Empowering AI: Azure Bootcamp

USET delivered an intensive 5-Day Microsoft AI Bootcamp, moving beyond theory to help students deploy Machine Learning models using Azure Cognitive Services. Over 100 students mastered cloud-based intelligence and ethical AI deployment.

L&T ACADEMY Product Strategy

Human-Centric Design Thinking

The L&T Product Design Thinking workshop transformed how students approach problem-solving. By focusing on the user journey—Empathize, Define, Ideate, and Prototype—engineers developed human-centric solutions for real-world industrial pain points.

FDP: Start-ups & IPR

In collaboration with NITTTR Chandigarh, USET hosted a 5-day Faculty Development Programme on "Business Start-ups and Intellectual Property Rights." Experts shared insights on translating research into impactful commercial ventures.

Train-to-Hire Program

The "Train-to-Hire" program was launched to provide final-year students with industry-specific skill sets. This initiative ensures that USET graduates are not just degree holders but corporate-ready professionals for global markets.

Visit: Sensation Software

An industrial visit to Sensation Software Solutions Pvt Ltd allowed students to witness real-time software development life cycles and interact with project managers to understand the current demands of the IT workforce.
