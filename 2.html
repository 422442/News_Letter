L&T Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) Training

USET successfully conducted the 4th batch of intensive training on Cyber-Physical Systems in collaboration with L&T. This program focuses on integrating computational algorithms with physical processes, preparing students for Industry 4.0. The hands-on sessions covered sensor networks, embedded systems, and real-time data processing, ensuring our engineers are ready for the complex demands of modern infrastructure.

L&T Product Design Thinking

Expert sessions on Design Thinking enabled students to approach engineering problems with a human-centric mindset, focusing on empathy, ideation, and rapid prototyping.
