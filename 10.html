SRIJANAM 3.0:
A Vibrant Dawn for Batch 2025

On 14th November 2025, the University School of Engineering & Technology (USET) at Lamrin Tech Skills University Punjab celebrated SRIJANAM 3.0, marking a vibrant beginning for the new academic session. The campus was transformed into a realm of glamour and celebration, welcoming the technical minds of the future with open arms.

"Innovation and collaboration are the dual engines of engineering. At SRIJANAM, we don't just welcome students; we welcome new ideas, new connections, and the promise of a developed Bharat."

— Leadership Address
